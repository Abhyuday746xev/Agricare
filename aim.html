Our Aim

Agricare is built with a mission to empower small and marginal farmers, many of whom remain unreached by conventional information systems. According to NABARD (2022) over 85% of Indian farmers are smallholders, cultivating less than 2 hectares of land. These farmers often lack access to timely, relevant advice, leading to suboptimal decisions, lower yields, and increased vulnerability to pests and weather shocks. Agricare aims to bridge this gap by providing personalized, actionable recommendations that are easy to understand and implement.

Currently, Agricare is focused on Punjab farmers, ensuring locally tailored recommendations and guidance. Our vision is to help every farmer make smarter decisions, reduce risks, and improve productivity sustainably.

By leveraging technology and expert knowledge, we aim to transform agricultural advisory into a practical, accessible service that truly meets farmers' needs.
